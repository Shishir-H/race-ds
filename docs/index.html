Fighting Covid-19

Our Safety Measures :-

  • Face mask and gloves are used all the times while giving training.

  • All the parts of the cars are disinfected by our trainers.

  • Daily washing, sanitizing and cleaning the cars.

  • Steering and gear will be sanitized after every class.

  • Daily body temperature check for all the trainers.

  • Mandatory use of Arogya Setu App by our staff.

We insit our customers :-

  • Mandatory use of Arogyasetu App

  • Wear facemask and gloves whenever you are taking class.

  • Wash your hands thoroughly before and after taking the class.

  • Use the sanitier kept in the car before and after the class.

  • Do not attend if you are not feeling well.

  • Mandatory use of Arogya Setu App by our staff.
